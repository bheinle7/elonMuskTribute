Tribute to Elon Musk

The Real-Life Tony Stark

1971

Elon is born in Pretoria South Africa

1981

Elon teaches himself how to code in Visual Basic.

1983

Elon creates and sells a video game for $500.

1988

Elon graduates high school and moves to Canada with his mother.

1995

Elon recieves his B.S. in physics from the University of Pennsylvania.

1995

Leaves Ph.D. program at Stanford to pursue his passions

1995

Elon and brother found Zip2 , a city guide company, and sell it for $34M in 1999

1999

Elon founds X.com, an online financial services and e-mail payment company

2000

X.com merges with Confinity and focuses on developing a service called PayPal

2002

Confinity was renammed to PayPal and is acquired by Ebay in 2002 for $1.5B

2002

Elon founds SpaceX , an aerospace manufacturer and space transport company

2004

Elon becomes chairman of Tesla Inc. , an automaker and energy storage company

2006

Elon provides intial concept and capital for SolarCity and is founded by his cousins.

2008

Elon becomes CEO and product architect of Tesla Inc., positions he still holds today

2013

Elon unveils the concept for a high-speed transportation system and calls it Hyperloop

2015

Elon and others found OpenAI, a non-profit that aims to develop AI safely and responsibly

2016

Elon, unveils idea to create underground tunnels for faster transportaion. Will he pursue?
